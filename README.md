# CRIT Climate Resilient School Infrastructure Toolkit

**Overview**

The Climate-Resilient School Infrastructure Toolkit (CRIT) is an open-source initiative by WUM Advisory Services designed to strengthen the resilience of school infrastructure against climate-related hazards such as floods, heatwaves, storms, and extreme rainfall.

CRIT supports governments, engineers, education planners, and communities in designing, retrofitting, and maintaining safe and climate-resilient learning environments for children.

Problem Context

School infrastructure in Pakistan is highly vulnerable to climate shocks, leading to repeated damage, school closures, and learning disruptions. Limited technical capacity and fragmented planning systems further worsen resilience gaps.

Solution

CRIT provides a standardized yet adaptable toolkit that includes:

Climate risk screening tools
School vulnerability assessment framework
Modular resilient school design templates
Retrofitting guidelines for existing schools
Costing and planning checklists
Community engagement tools
Monitoring and evaluation guidance
Key Outcomes
Reduced school closures due to climate events
Improved structural safety of school buildings
Lower long-term rehabilitation costs
Strengthened local planning and technical capacity
Enhanced continuity of learning in crisis contexts
Target Users
Education departments (provincial & district)
Engineers and infrastructure planners
NGOs and humanitarian organizations
School communities and management committees
Development partners and donors
Status

CRIT is currently at an advanced prototype stage and is ready for pilot implementation in climate-vulnerable districts.
