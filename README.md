## 📌 Overview
The **Climate-Resilient School Infrastructure Toolkit (CRIT)** is an open-source, scalable toolkit developed by **WUM Advisory Services** to strengthen school infrastructure resilience against climate-related hazards such as **floods, heatwaves, storms, and extreme rainfall**.

CRIT supports governments, engineers, education planners, humanitarian actors, and communities to plan, design, retrofit, and maintain **safe, climate-resilient learning environments** for children.

---

## 🚸 Problem Context
Pakistan’s public education infrastructure is increasingly exposed to climate shocks. Each year, climate-induced disasters damage or disrupt schools, resulting in:
- prolonged school closures  
- learning loss and dropout risks  
- unsafe learning environments  
- repeated reconstruction cycles and high rehabilitation costs  

This disproportionately affects children in rural and underserved communities, deepening educational inequities.

---

## 💡 The CRIT Solution
CRIT provides a **standardized yet locally adaptable framework** for climate-resilient school infrastructure planning and implementation.

It transforms fragmented infrastructure responses into a **proactive, evidence-based, and system-strengthening approach** aligned with global safe schools principles.

---

## 🧰 What the Toolkit Includes
CRIT is structured into practical modules such as:

✅ **Climate Risk Screening Tools**  
✅ **School Vulnerability Scoring & Prioritization Framework**  
✅ **Modular Climate-Resilient Design Templates**  
✅ **Retrofitting Guidance for Existing Schools**  
✅ **Flood Protection & Drainage Planning Tools**  
✅ **Heat Mitigation and Passive Cooling Solutions**  
✅ **Costing Tools and Implementation Checklists**  
✅ **Community Engagement Tools for Ownership & Maintenance**  
✅ **Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) Framework for Resilience Outcomes**

---

## 🎯 Target Users
CRIT is designed for:
- Provincial and district **education departments**
- **engineers, architects, and infrastructure planners**
- NGOs and humanitarian organizations
- school communities and management committees
- development partners and donors

---

## 📈 Expected Outcomes
CRIT contributes to:
- **reduced climate-related school closure days**
- improved structural safety and disaster preparedness
- reduced long-term rehabilitation and reconstruction costs
- strengthened local technical capacity
- improved continuity of learning during emergencies
- equitable access to safer learning spaces for vulnerable children

---

## 🚀 Implementation Approach
CRIT supports implementation through a structured process:

1. **Hazard screening and climate risk mapping**
2. **Infrastructure vulnerability assessment**
3. **Prioritization of high-risk schools**
4. **Selection of resilient design/retrofit modules**
5. **Costing and phased implementation planning**
6. **Training of district teams and local implementers**
7. **Pilot retrofits and monitoring of results**

---
