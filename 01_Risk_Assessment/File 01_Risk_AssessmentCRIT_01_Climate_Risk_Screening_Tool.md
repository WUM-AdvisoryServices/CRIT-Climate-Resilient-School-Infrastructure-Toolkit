# CRIT Module 01: Climate Risk Screening Tool (School-Level)

## Purpose
This tool supports rapid identification of climate and disaster risks affecting school infrastructure and learning continuity. It enables district education teams and local engineers to classify schools into risk categories and prioritize interventions.

## Users
- District Education Officers (DEO)
- School Infrastructure Departments / Works Departments
- Engineers and planners
- School Management Committees (SMCs)
- NGOs and implementing partners

## Step 6: Risk Classification
| Composite Score | Risk Level | Recommended Action |
|----------------|-----------|-------------------|
| 0–10 | Low | Monitoring + minor repairs |
| 11–20 | Medium | Planned retrofitting package |
| 21–30 | High | Priority retrofitting + DRR planning |
| 31+ | Critical | Immediate safety intervention / relocation assessment |
